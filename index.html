Services

My process is effective and delivers the results you are looking for: competitive edge for your service or product. Let me describe to you, how I will apply my knowledge to wield the perfect results for your needs.

Research

With every project I select I will proceed methodically. Knowing about the service or product offered by the company, understanding their goal, is of the utmost importance, which will lead to more accurate results determined by market research.

Questionning

Conducting interviews are a integral part of user satisfaction. Phrasing the user's mindset, helps represent their interaction with the interface and develop an accurate model to frame the difficulties they are facing. In doing so, creating for them a better experience.

Concepts

Communication with the stakeholder is a priority. Giving feedback about my findings and especially receiving their vues and ideation will lead to a better design and problem solving.

Prototyping

This process will facilitate the visualisation of the final solution. Putting pen to paper by sketching and wireframing, building with every phase of the project, a prototype which will be tested along the process with real users.

Developing

With all of this testing done, I will be able to deploy the best concept available for the product or service to introduce to the futur users. Either web based or ios, I will finalise the project and follow through with adjustments along the first few month of active deployment.
